Design. Build. Share. Observe.

Solyd Solutions is a maker-driven hub for CNC files, CAD/CAM workflows, and laser/metal/woodworking plans. We integrate analytics, observability, and econometrics to measure performance, optimize production, and connect academic modeling with practical maker systems.

What You Will Find Here

CNC Router and Laser Files

SVG, DXF, and toolpath-ready formats for routers and lasers.

CAD/CAM Models

Parametric parts designed in SolidWorks, Fusion 360, and OpenSCAD.

Analytics and Observability

Google Cloud and Splunk observability projects, dashboards, and monitoring templates. Data-driven optimization for CNC, laser, and upholstery workflows.

Econometrics

Reproducible regression models, simulations, and forecasting. Bridges academic economics with practical maker data to support grant writing, business planning, and product pricing strategies.

Documentation and Brand Assets

Setup notes, material guidelines, best practices for small-batch manufacturing, and Solyd Solutions branding.

Getting Started

1. Clone the repository to access files and documentation.

2. Review the /docs folder for CNC and laser setup notes.

3. Explore /cad_cam, /gcode, /analytics, and /econometrics for designs, toolpaths, dashboards, and models.
